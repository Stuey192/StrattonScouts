Beaver, Cubs & Scouts

If you think Scouting’s just about knots, woggles and big shorts, then be prepared to be surprised. It’s all about adventure, new experiences and great memories.

From archery and acting to street sports and zorbing, being a Scout gives you the chance to get stuck into loads of activities you wouldn’t normally get to do. As a Scout, you get to explore the big outdoors and travel to new places and try new things. Sounds better than being stuck in the house with nothing to do, right?
